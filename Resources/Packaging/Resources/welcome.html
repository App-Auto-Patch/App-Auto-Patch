App Auto-Patch

App Auto-Patch is an MDM-agnostic third-party patching tool that combines local application discovery, an Installomator integration, and user-friendly swiftDialog prompts to automate application patch management across your Mac fleet.

Clicking Install installs App Auto-Patch in the background — no further action is required here. Once installation completes, App Auto-Patch registers itself as a LaunchDaemon and, depending on your managed configuration, may immediately run its first application discovery and patching workflow.

For configuration options and documentation, visit the App Auto-Patch Wiki.
